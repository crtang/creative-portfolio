About the Composer

Tang Rui Ting

tahng ray-ting

I'm a pianist with a love for cinematic music and stories. Though I was classically trained, I grew up composing piano covers of my favorite songs. I also played two years of flute in a school band. After developing arm problems toward the end of high school and several years of recovery, I eventually turned to writing and creating my own music, which I sometimes combine with the sounds of instruments from my Chinese heritage. I listen to alternative, indie, cinematic, and Christian, with a sprinkling of songs from other languages.
