About the Composer

Tang Rui Ting

tahng ray-ting

Tang Rui Ting is a pianist with a love for cinematic music and stories. Though she was classically trained, she also grew up composing piano covers of her favorite songs. After developing arm problems toward the end of high school and spending several years recovering, she eventually turned to writing and creating her own music, which she sometimes combines with the sounds of instruments from her Chinese heritage.
